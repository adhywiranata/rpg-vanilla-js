Fantasy World: The Dark King

Lagendia is a prosperous and peaceful kingdom. But one day, wyverns came in and ruin the kingdom. It is up to three brave adventurers to stop the wyverns' attacks.

Your Goal is to Defeat the Wyvernos, the King of Wyverns.






You have three person on your party:

  • Megumin, the archmage who specializes in explosion magic

    Megumin Skills

    • Explosion: Deal heavy damage to the enemy
    • Mega Blast: Deal insane damage to the enemy
    • Mana Recharge: Awaken all skills, ignore its cooldowns

  • Aqua, the archpriest who specializes in healing magic

    Aqua Skills

    • Smash: Deal small damage to the enemy
    • Heal: Heal small amount of HP to party (also revives dead member)
    • Salvation: Fully heal HP of the party

  • Kazuma, the thief who specializes in melee and Life Drain

    Kazuma Skills

    • Stab: Deal moderate damage to the enemy
    • Heal: Deal moderate damage to the enemy and heal himself
    • Dakunes' Shield: All party members cannot be damaged for 10 seconds
